Welcome to our landing page

Hi. We are a group of teens that attended the Christian Media International Media Camp in June 2016, and we had a blast. We had opportunities to get hands-on experience with video, graphic design, and web development; and best of all we were provoked and inspired to use our talents and abilities for the furtherance of the Gospel.

We would like to take this opportunity to share with you some things that we’ve learned, created and taken away at camp this year.

Who we are

We are a group of 10 teenagers that came from Lighthouse Baptist Church in Dawsonville, Georgia. We raised $3,500 to come to Media Camp.
